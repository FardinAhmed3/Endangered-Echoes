Install the App

Endangered Echoes is a Progressive Web App that works on any device with a modern browser.

  1. Open the app in Chrome, Edge, Safari, or any modern browser
  2. Click the "Install App" button in the application
  3. Follow the browser prompts to install
  4. Access the app from your home screen or app drawer
